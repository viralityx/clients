04 — What We Build

The Full Operational Stack

We don't sell chatbots. We go inside your operation, map your processes, and build the infrastructure that lets your team focus on closing — not chasing.

Phase 1 — Command Center

CRM, Pipeline & Automation Engine

Before any AI touches a lead, you need a structured system that knows where every prospect is and what happens next — automatically. This is the backbone of everything.

Custom CRM Build

Pipeline stages mapped to your credit repair funnel: New Lead → Qualified → DIY Buyer → Upsell Candidate → Done-For-You Client → Active Case → Completed.

Automated Follow-Up Sequences

SMS + email nurture triggered by lead behavior. Bought the DIY kit? Upsell sequence fires. Called but didn't book? Re-engagement starts automatically.

Lead Scoring Dashboard

Every lead scored: hot, warm, cold. Your team sees exactly who to call first based on engagement, intent signals, and data collected by the AI agent.

Appointment Booking System

Done-for-you consultation leads self-book directly into your team's calendar. Automated confirmations and reminders eliminate no-shows.

Phase 2 — AI Voice Agent

24/7 Inbound Call Handling & Qualification

When a podcast drops and 500 people call in the same day, your AI agent picks up every single one — qualifies them, captures their data, and routes them to the right place.

Inbound AI Voice Agent

Natural-sounding agent trained on your credit repair services, pricing tiers, and FAQ. Handles unlimited concurrent calls. No hold times. No missed revenue.

Intelligent Lead Qualification

On-call intake collects name, credit score range, issue types (collections, bankruptcies, liens, late payments), and budget — scoring intent in real time.

Smart Routing

DIY-ready leads get directed to checkout. High-value prospects get booked into live consultations automatically. The right path for every caller, every time.

Call Analytics & Grading

Every call transcribed, scored, and tagged. See which ones are hot, what objections surface, and what patterns emerge across your entire inbound volume.

Phase 3 — Revenue Engine

Upsells, Partner Tracking & Growth Infrastructure

This is where compounding starts. Every DIY buyer becomes a potential high-ticket client. Every podcaster becomes a trackable revenue source. Every car lot becomes a referral channel.

Post-Purchase Upsell Sequences

Automated SMS + email after DIY purchase: educational content → pain point agitation → done-for-you offer → booking link. Converts buyers into high-ticket clients on autopilot.

AI Chat Widget on Your Website

Embedded on mycreditscrub.com — answers visitor questions in real time, handles objections, and pushes qualified prospects to purchase or book consultations.

Referral & Partner Attribution

Unique tracking for every podcaster, influencer, and used car lot partner. Know exactly which source generates which revenue — critical for scaling your network.

Revenue Intelligence Dashboard

Real-time view of DIY sales, upsell conversions, call volume, close rates, and revenue by source. Stop guessing — start knowing where to double down.
