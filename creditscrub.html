02 — The Solution

A Full Operational Stack — Not Just a Chatbot

We don't sell tools. We go inside your operation, map your processes, and build the infrastructure that lets your team focus on closing — not chasing.

Phase 1 — Foundation

CRM, Pipeline & Automation Engine

The backbone of everything. Before any AI touches a lead, you need a structured system that knows where every prospect is and what happens next — automatically.

Custom CRM Build

Pipeline stages mapped to your credit repair funnel: New Lead → Qualified → DIY Buyer → Upsell Candidate → Done-For-You Client → Active Case → Completed.

Automated Follow-Up Sequences

SMS + email nurture sequences triggered by lead behavior — purchased DIY kit? They get the upsell sequence. Called but didn't book? They get re-engaged.

Lead Scoring Dashboard

Every lead scored automatically: hot, warm, cold. Your team sees exactly who to call first based on engagement, intent signals, and qualification data.

Appointment Booking System

For done-for-you consultations — leads can self-book directly into your team's calendar with automated confirmations and reminders.

Phase 2 — AI Voice Agent

24/7 Inbound Call Handling & Qualification

When a podcast drops and 500 people call in the same day, your AI agent picks up every single one — qualifies them, captures their data, and routes them into the right pipeline stage.

Inbound AI Voice Agent

Natural-sounding agent trained on your credit repair services. Answers questions about DIY kits, pricing, and done-for-you packages. Handles unlimited concurrent calls.

Lead Qualification Survey

On-call intake: collects name, credit score range, types of issues (collections, bankruptcies, liens, late payments), and budget. Scores intent in real time.

Smart Routing

DIY-ready leads get directed to the website to purchase. High-value leads ($750+) get booked into a live consultation automatically. No lead gets lost.

Call Analytics & Grading

Every call transcribed, scored, and tagged. See which ones are hot, which need follow-up, and what patterns emerge across your inbound volume.

Phase 3 — Upsell Engine

Turn $29.99 Buyers into $1,500 Clients

The highest-ROI part of this entire build. Your existing buyers are your warmest leads — they already trust you enough to pull out their card.

Post-Purchase Upsell Sequence

Automated SMS + email flow triggered after DIY kit purchase. Educational content → pain point agitation → done-for-you offer → booking link.

AI Chat Widget on Website

Embedded on mycreditscrub.com — answers questions, handles objections ("is it worth $750?"), and pushes qualified visitors to book a consultation or purchase.

Referral & Partner Tracking

Unique tracking links for podcasters, influencers, and used car lot partners. See exactly which source drives which revenue — critical for your referral play.

Revenue Dashboard

Real-time view of DIY sales, upsell conversions, call volume, and revenue by source. Know exactly what's working and where to double down.
