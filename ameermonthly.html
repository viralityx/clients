In Progress

What We're Fixing

Direct Traffic Quality Issue

45% of sessions show as "Direct" but with only 30% engagement and 18s average time — well below organic's 68%. This pattern suggests bot or spam traffic. We're investigating and will implement filters to clean this data.

FAQ Page Underperforming

The /faq page shows only 6 seconds of engagement — visitors aren't finding answers. We're rewriting the content with real patient questions and detailed, helpful answers that keep visitors engaged.

About Page Bounce

The /about page averages just 2 seconds — essentially an immediate bounce. We're rewriting this with compelling content about Coral Med's mission, approach, and what makes the practice different.

Zero Conversion Tracking

Currently, we can't measure how many visitors actually become patients. We're setting up phone click tracking and form submission events so we can report on real patient inquiries — not just pageviews.

SEO Title Tags Need Deployment

Current page titles aren't optimized for search rankings. We've already written the new titles and meta descriptions — they're ready to deploy on Replit this week.
