Health Tip of the Month

Summer in Huntington Beach: 3 easy wins

•  Hydrate before you're thirsty — aim for pale-yellow, not clear or dark.
•  Sunscreen is preventive medicine: SPF 30+, reapply every 2 hours outdoors.
•  Heat and blood-pressure meds don't always mix — check in if you feel lightheaded.

Why It Matters

Why having a primary care doctor is worth it

A good primary care doctor is the one who knows your history, catches the small things before they become big ones, and helps you navigate the rest of the system. An annual wellness visit is the simplest way to stay ahead of your health — most take just minutes and can flag issues long before you'd feel them. If it's been a while, consider this your nudge.
