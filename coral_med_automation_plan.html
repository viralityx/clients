03 🚨 viralityX forms & PHI — non-negotiable
Hard rule

No BAA exists with viralityX. viralityX forms cannot be used for any patient-facing intake, registration, or anything that collects PHI.

  • New patient registration forms in viralityX
  • Any viralityX form asking for DOB, insurance, address, or medical history
  • Any viralityX survey or intake questionnaire
Form typeWhere it livesSafe?
New patient intake, demographics, medical historyPatient Ally / Intake Pro only✓ Safe
HIPAA acknowledgment, consent to treatPatient Ally / Intake Pro only✓ Safe
Insurance info, financial responsibilityPatient Ally / Intake Pro only✓ Safe
Generic "request an appointment" (name + phone only)viralityX✓ Safe
Any form with DOB, insurance, or clinical infoNever in viralityX without BAA🚨 Prohibited
Implementation note

The VA must be explicitly told: viralityX forms are for lead capture only — name and phone number. Everything clinical goes through Patient Ally. This rule must live in the VA's written SOP and onboarding checklist — not just communicated verbally. A verbal instruction will not hold.

04 ⚠️ Walk-in / phone patient fallback

If a walk-in or phone patient is created directly in Practice Mate, the viralityX trigger never fires and the sync is missed.

Recommended — start here
Option A — SOP rule

VA always creates in viralityX first, even for walk-ins. Simple, no extra build required. Relies on VA discipline.

Safety net — add if needed
Option B — Dual trigger

Second Keragon workflow triggered by Practice Mate webhook or manual VA form for walk-ins. More resilient, slightly more steps.

VA SOP rule: "Every new patient — walk-in, phone, or scheduled — must be created in viralityX first before entering Practice Mate. No exceptions."

06 Invoice creation — verified with caveats

The Keragon Office Ally connector supports Create Invoice and Update Invoice — confirmed.

What it doesWhat it doesn't do
✓ Creates an invoice record in Office Ally✗ Does not auto-populate CPT codes
✓ Accepts invoice content as a text string✗ Does not pull diagnosis codes from the chart
✓ Returns an invoice ID for tracking✗ Does not submit the claim to the payer

Expectation to set with Coral Med: "Keragon creates the invoice record automatically. Your VA still adds the billing codes and submits the claim inside Office Ally. If a visit is marked complete in error, the VA voids the invoice directly in Office Ally."

Invoice error guardrail

Because Keragon fires immediately on every "visit complete" trigger, a guardrail is needed.

Recommended — start here
Option B — Delay buffer

10–15 minute sleep step in Keragon before invoice is created. Passive, no extra VA action required. Catches most errors. Upgrade to Option A if errors become a pattern.

Upgrade if errors recur
Option A — Confirmation step

Keragon sends VA a confirmation SMS/email before creating the invoice. VA replies "YES" to confirm. Best for new VAs or practices with occasional errors.
