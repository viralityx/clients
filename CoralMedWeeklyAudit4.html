Google Search clicks increased from 51 to 60 in the 28-day rolling window. Impressions also grew 12% (758 to 849). The GBP optimization and SEO changes are starting to take effect — more people are seeing and clicking through to the site.

Search Console shows both coral-med.com and www.coral-med.com as separate pages — the homepage alone is split into 8 clicks + 6 clicks across two URLs. This means Google sees them as different pages and is dividing ranking power between them. A canonical redirect will consolidate this.

Technical Issue: www vs. Non-www Split

The homepage is appearing as two separate URLs in Google's index (coral-med.com and www.coral-med.com). Combined, it would show 14 clicks and 164 impressions — but Google is splitting the ranking authority between them. Setting up a 301 redirect from one version to the other will consolidate all ranking signals into a single URL, which should improve position and CTR.
