AI Voice Agent + Missed Call Recovery
ViralityX CRM
▼
AI voice agent setup
Configure AI Conversation Agent for inbound calls
Train on FAQs: hours (Mon–Fri 8:30–5:30), location, insurance, services
Enable appointment booking capability through the agent
Set fallback to voicemail if AI cannot handle the request
Missed call text-back

Patient replies → auto-create contact + notify staff
No reply in 2 hours → send follow-up text

6
Office Ally ↔ ViralityX CRM Data Bridge
Custom Solution
▼
Sync workflows to build
New patient in Office Ally → create contact in ViralityX CRM
Confirmed lead in ViralityX CRM → create patient record in Office Ally
Appointment status change in Office Ally → update CRM pipeline stage
Appointment completed → move to "Active Patient" in CRM
No-show in Office Ally → trigger CRM no-show follow-up workflow
Patient inactive 90+ days → tag as "Reactivation" in CRM

Digital Intake Forms
ViralityX CRMOffice Ally
▼
Build in ViralityX CRM
Digital intake form: demographics, insurance info, medical history, consent

Form data auto-populates contact record
Sync to Office Ally
Completed intake data flows to Office Ally patient record via custom solution
Staff reviews in Office Ally before appointment — no manual data entry
8
Patient Reactivation Campaign
ViralityX CRM
▼
Automated sequence
Trigger: patient tagged "Inactive – Reactivation" (90+ days since last visit)
Day 1: personalized SMS nudge to re-engage
Day 3: email with health tips + gentle reminder to schedule
Day 7: follow-up SMS — "Your health matters to us"
Day 14: final SMS with appointment availability this week
Patient books → removed from campaign, moved to "Appointment Scheduled"
