Recommendations

How We Improve From Here

Based on this audit, here are the highest-impact actions we can take to grow your website traffic and convert more visitors into booked patients.

01
High Priority

Resolve Google Business Profile Verification

Your GBP still shows the wrong city due to an incomplete video verification. This is the single biggest blocker for local SEO. A verified GBP with correct info will unlock Google Maps visibility, the local 3-pack, and dramatically increase organic search traffic from Huntington Beach patients.

02
High Priority

Launch Google Review Collection Engine

With only 12 reviews currently, every new 5-star review significantly impacts your local ranking. We are building an automated post-visit review request system that will make this effortless. Goal: reach 30+ reviews within 90 days to outpace local competitors.

03
High Priority

Build Out Service-Specific Landing Pages

Right now, 78% of traffic lands on the homepage. Creating dedicated, SEO-optimized pages for key services (annual wellness exams, same-day sick visits, immigration physicals, weight management) will help each page rank independently on Google — multiplying your organic search entry points.

04
Medium Priority

Optimize Page Titles and Meta Descriptions

Several page titles are long and repetitive. Clean, keyword-focused titles and compelling meta descriptions for each page will improve click-through rates in Google search results. Target keywords: "primary care Huntington Beach," "doctor near me accepting new patients," "same-day appointment Huntington Beach."

05
Medium Priority

Increase Form and Call-to-Action Conversions

Only 2 form starts were recorded in the past week. Adding prominent "Book Now" or "Request an Appointment" CTAs on every page — especially the doctor page and services page — will turn passive browsers into booked patients. We are already building this into the website update sprint.

06
Medium Priority

Launch Local Blog Content Strategy

Publishing 2–3 SEO-optimized blog posts per month targeting local health questions (e.g., "best primary care doctor in Huntington Beach," "what to expect at an annual wellness visit") will build topical authority and drive long-tail organic search traffic to the site.

07
Ongoing

Directory Citations and NAP Cleanup

Ensuring the practice name, address, and phone number are consistent across Healthgrades, Zocdoc, Vitals, Yelp, and other directories strengthens Google's trust in your business listing and improves local search rankings. This is planned as part of our Sprint 3 buildout.

08
Ongoing

Set Up Conversion Tracking (Key Events)

Google Analytics currently shows zero key events. We will configure conversion tracking for form submissions, phone clicks, and appointment bookings so we can measure exactly how many website visitors become patients — and which channels produce the best results.
