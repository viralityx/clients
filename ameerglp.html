The Science

What are GLP-1 medications?

GLP-1 medications work with your body's own biology. They mimic a natural hormone that signals fullness to your brain and slows how quickly your stomach empties — so you feel satisfied sooner, stay full longer, and naturally eat less without white-knuckling through hunger. In clinical trials, patients on these medications have seen meaningful, sustained weight loss when paired with lifestyle changes.

Questions

Frequently asked

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Results vary by person, medication, and how closely the plan is followed. In clinical studies, patients on GLP-1 medications combined with lifestyle changes have lost a significant percentage of their body weight over time. Dr. Moussa will set realistic expectations for your situation at your visit.

What are the side effects?

The most common side effects are gastrointestinal — nausea, constipation, or diarrhea — and they're usually mild and tend to ease as your body adjusts. We start low and increase your dose gradually to minimize them, and we're here to help manage anything that comes up.
