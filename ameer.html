Keyword Analysis & Opportunities

Where the biggest SEO wins are hiding
Quick Win

"coral med" — 63 Impressions, Only 3.2% CTR

This is the #1 impression keyword but position 5.0 means other "Coral Med" brands are competing. The branded search result title needs to clearly say "Primary Care — Huntington Beach" to stand out and earn the click.

Quick Win

"weight loss huntington beach" — Position 2.5

Ranking near the top for a high-value service keyword but getting 0 clicks. The page title and meta description for the weight loss service page needs to be compelling enough to earn the click at this position.

Strong Position

"direct primary care orange county" — #1

Already ranking #1 with a 50% CTR. This is a validated keyword. We should build more content around DPC/concierge medicine to dominate this search category across related queries.

Needs Work

Address Searches — Position 9, 0% CTR

"17122 beach blvd" has 15 impressions but 0 clicks at position 9. This means the GBP listing isn't showing up strongly for address-based searches. The GBP updates we made this week should help improve this.

Gap

Missing: Non-Branded Discovery Keywords

Not ranking for critical searches like "primary care doctor huntington beach," "family doctor near me," "doctor accepting new patients huntington beach." These are the highest-intent searches and the biggest growth opportunity.

Strategy

Target Keywords for This Month

Priority targets: "primary care huntington beach," "doctor huntington beach," "family doctor fountain valley," "medical weight loss orange county," "concierge doctor huntington beach." These need dedicated page content.

This Week's Action Plan

Priority tasks for May 19–25, 2026
  • 1
    Deploy SEO page titles & meta descriptions on coral-med.com
    The optimized titles and metas are ready (document already created). Deploying them on Replit will immediately improve CTR for "coral med" (currently 3.2%) and help rank for non-branded keywords. This is the single highest-impact SEO action right now.
  • 2
    Create dedicated landing pages for target keywords
    Build out pages for: "Weight Loss Huntington Beach" (already ranking #2.5), "Direct Primary Care Orange County" (already #1), and "Primary Care Huntington Beach" (not ranking yet). Each page needs 500+ words of unique content targeting that keyword.
  • 3
    Deploy /checkup and /wellness QR code redirects on Replit
    Only /welcome is live. The other two postcard QR codes need their redirect pages built so all three mailer campaigns are tracking in GA.
  • 4
    Upload 10+ photos to Google Business Profile
    Dr. Moussa needs to provide: professional headshot, office exterior, waiting room, exam rooms, team photo, and signage. Businesses with more photos get significantly more calls and direction requests.
  • 5
    Set up GA4 conversion tracking
    Configure events for phone number clicks, contact form submissions, and appointment bookings. This lets us measure which traffic sources actually convert into patients.
  • 6
    Weekly Google Post
    Posts expire after 7 days. Create a new post each week highlighting a different service, health tip, or patient-facing update. No phone numbers in text — use the "Call" button instead.
  • 7
    Review generation strategy
    15 reviews at 5.0 stars is great quality but low volume. Implement a review request flow — after appointments, send patients to the Google review link. Target: 5 new reviews per month.
